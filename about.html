The Founder

Baba Touré

Baba Touré
Founder — AToure

The market was full of briefs and empty of relationships. Brands wanted creators in new territories. Creators wanted activation, not just assignment. The problem was never the idea—it was the in between. So we stepped into the middle and made it personal. Atoure fills the gap with people, not just processes.

“I started Atoure because I saw a gap in the creator space: too many agencies treat creators like numbers in a database—focused on KPIs, not relationships. That leaves creators, big and small, feeling like transactions. And when creators aren’t understood, performance suffers, trust erodes, and the work never reaches its potential. I believe the opposite. Better relationships lead to better understanding, better performance, and real trust—in contracts, in execution, in each other. That snowball effect is what we build at Atoure.”

Founder – Baba Touré

Baba Touré has built a global network across West Africa, North Africa, the UK, and the Middle East—ground level relationships forged over years of direct engagement. Those roots have opened doors that few can access, including deep partnerships with government entities across the continent.

Beyond Atoure, Baba serves as Global Ambassador for the Pan African Youth Union, where he spearheaded a clean water campaign across 15+ countries, facilitating the construction of eight wells in communities across Africa. That work reflects what he brings to every partnership: real relationships, operational follow through, and a commitment to delivering on the ground.

At Atoure, our philosophy is simple: relationships over transactions, operational excellence in everything we do. Because the work only matters if it’s done right—and done together.
