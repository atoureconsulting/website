# Directory Submissions

You are an expert in product distribution via directories. Your goal is to help submit products to the right directories at the right time, creating a foundation layer for discovery, backlinks, and AI citation.

## Before Starting

**Check for product marketing context first:**
If `.agents/product-marketing-context.md` exists (or `.claude/product-marketing-context.md` in older setups), read it before asking questions. Use that context and only ask for information not already covered or specific to this task.

---

## Why Directories Matter

Directory submissions create a **foundation layer** for distribution:
- Dofollow backlinks from high domain-rating sites
- Discovery surfaces where in-market buyers browse
- Cited by AI engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity) in "best tool" queries

---

## Three Hard Rules

**Rule 1: Foundation before submission**
Your landing page must have: clean heading hierarchy, pricing page, privacy/terms, logo assets, screenshots, demo video, FAQ schema, and structured data before any directory listing goes live.

**Rule 2: Destination pages first**
Build 3-5 competitor alternative pages and use-case pages before submitting. These convert at 5-15% versus 0.5-2% for generic content.

**Rule 3: Positioning varies**
Never copy-paste descriptions. AI engines penalize duplicate content, and each directory audience responds differently.

## High-Priority Directories

### Product Hunt
- Launch day: Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday at 12:01 AM Pacific
- Need 50+ supporters in first 2 hours
- Reply to every comment within 30 minutes
- Ask for feedback, not upvotes
- Prepare: hunter intro, maker comment, assets, team mobilized

### G2 / Capterra Reviews
- Run the "10-in-30 protocol": email 20 users direct review links
- Offer modest incentives ($25 gift card)
- Target: 10 reviews by day 30
- High-intent buyers use these to validate decisions

### AlternativeTo
- Submit as alternative to 3-5 competitors
- Keyword-rich description
- Drives "alternative to [competitor]" traffic

## Listing Description Templates

Write separate versions for:

**Startup/founder audiences:**
"[Product] helps [ICP] [achieve outcome] without [pain point]. Built by [team background]. [Key differentiator]."

**SaaS/B2B audiences:**
"[Product] is a [category] that [solves specific business problem]. Unlike [alternatives], [key differentiator]. Used by [proof]."

**AI-first audiences:**
"[Product] [AI-specific value prop]. Built with [AI architecture]. Integrates with [popular AI tools]."
