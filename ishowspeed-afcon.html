The Brief

A global creator. Africa’s biggest stage.

CAF and the Royal Moroccan Football Federation wanted to integrate a global creator into the AFCON 2025 Final to amplify the tournament’s reach into Gen Z globally — and they needed a partner who could execute it at the highest level. They came to Atoure.

What AToure Did

Conceived, negotiated, and delivered.

01
The Mascot Concept

AToure conceived the idea to appoint IShowSpeed as the official AFCON mascot “Assad”. CAF and the Royal Moroccan Football Federation reviewed and signed off — but the concept was ours.

02
State-Level Access

Secured state-sponsored visas, official border clearances, and private jet clearances for the full team. VIP pickup from the private terminal in Rabat.

03
Permits & Equipment

Obtained all filming permits, equipment permits, and drone access permits across Morocco — every credential needed to operate at the Final.

04
Security

Arranged both private security and state security mobilisation for the entire tour — from Casablanca to the AFCON Final in Rabat.

05
Accommodation

Placed the full team at the top hotel in Rabat. Managed all hospitality, transport, and logistics from arrival through the Final.

06
The Final

Secured IShowSpeed’s entry to the AFCON Final and coordinated the live mascot reveal — the moment that drove 16M views and broke the internet.

The Result

One of the most-watched creator moments of 2026.

IShowSpeed’s mascot reveal at the AFCON Final

The stadium erupted as Speed was unveiled as Assad the lion. The moment went viral across Africa and globally, driving a record 6.1 billion digital impressions and 5.2 billion video views for the tournament — numbers that had never been seen before in AFCON history.
